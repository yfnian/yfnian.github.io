Research

In general, my research agenda focuses on agricultural sustainability issue in the food system. I believe that my work should not be determined by a fixed set of methods, but by the questions that are relevant and interesting to me and my stakeholders. Because of this, I have been preparing myself by continually developing a larger-than-typical toolbox.
