Teaching

I believe an instructor should “tailors the teaching method to each individuals’ aptitude, personality and interest (Confucius).” It is important to foster students’ economic reasoning and help them gain the ability to apply knowledge in real-life settings. I aim to create an environment in my courses where students fell comfortable, believe in themselves, not be afraid of making mistakes, and feel empowered in their ability to learn new knowledge.
